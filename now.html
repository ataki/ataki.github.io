Leadership and Volunteering

In the spare time I have, I'm trying to give back in two ways. One is leadership training for Asian Americans in tech. There's a lack of representation in leadership roles for Asian Americans. It's getting better, but there's a culture gap, especially for managers coming from overseas. Our goal is to establish a set of common principles across industries to bridge that cultural gap. The second is volunteering. I'm open to tutoring and coaching opportunities for pre-college kids, and new grads looking to get into the engineering market. If you'd like to speak, send me an invite
