Leadership and Volunteering

In the spare time I have, I'm trying to give back in two ways. One is leadership training for Asian Americans in tech. There's a disproportionate lack of representation in tech company leadership roles. Part of the reason is a culture and communication gap, especially for managers coming from overseas. Other reasons, perhaps harder to grasp, are stereotypes and representation. Our goal is to establish a set of common principles across industries to address the root causes. The second is volunteering. I'm open to tutoring and coaching opportunities for pre-college kids, and new grads looking to get into the engineering market. If you'd like to speak, send me an invite
