Hi, I'm Jim.

I'm currently building out data infrastructure at Invoice2go. Before that, I led design and engineering efforts at AnalyticsMD, supported the OpenEdx movement, and stuck around long enough to get a BS and Masters at Stanford. I've had fun and learned a lot about myself while living abroad in Shanghai, Osaka, and Zurich.

I've been involved in the startup scene for a bit, starting from 2012-13 at StartX. I held an advisory role at Boomtown Accelerator, and had fun trying to make my own.
