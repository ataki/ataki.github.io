HI I'M JIM

I'll keep my life story short.

I was born in China and immigrated to NY, then again to the Bay Area, where I've been living on and off for almost a decade.

I'm currently helping cook up data at Invoice2go. Before that, I led engineering at AnalyticsMD, supported the OpenEdx movement, and stuck around long enough to get a BS and Masters at Stanford. I've had fun and learned a lot about myself while living abroad in Shanghai, Osaka, and Zurich.

I've been involved in the startup scene for a bit, starting from 2012-13 at StartX. I held an advisory role at Boomtown Accelerator, and had fun trying to make my own in Steve Blank's Lean Launchpad class.

I love beautiful code and crafted products. Beautiful code is concise and expressive. Beautifully crafted products train me well at using them and fit neatly into a user's lifestyle. In both, there is an attention to detail that gets noticed over time and use.
